This [[Entities|Entity]] represents the body of a creature. It's its main way to absorb [[Mana]] and is able to store some. This [[Entities|Entity]] is affected by damages on the physical plane. If this [[Entities|Entity]] is eliminated, it kills the creature, freeing it's [[Spirit Entity]], it can then be captured up to a minute after provided someone around has the required tools for this task.
